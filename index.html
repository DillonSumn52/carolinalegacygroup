Our Story

Built to Consult.
Driven to Scale.

Founded in 2006 and rooted in the Greenville market, Carolina Legacy Group was built on a simple but powerful idea: that the right strategy and the right operator can transform an ordinary business into a market leader. We work with business owners who are ambitious, capable, and ready — but need a partner with the experience and bandwidth to help them break through their ceiling.

What separates us from traditional consultants is that we're also operators. We own and run businesses ourselves — across real estate, construction, media, and experiential spaces. That means every recommendation we make is grounded in what actually works in the real world, not just on paper.

Today we serve clients across Greenville and the Carolinas — helping businesses in a wide range of industries sharpen their operations, scale their revenue, and build a deeper, more durable presence in the markets they care about most.
